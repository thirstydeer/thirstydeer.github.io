Wonderbox is an atmospheric game combining basic principles of both 2D platformer and puzzle games. It is developed within a Computer Games Development (2017/18) course at Faculty of Mathematics and Physics, Charles University.

In her room, little Emmy opens mysterious box of unknown origin and through that, without knowing it, she transforms the world around her into void city desert inhabited solely by her drawings, which she has to overcome, because they seem to embody her fears somehow. You can download it!
